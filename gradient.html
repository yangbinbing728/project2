Gradient Highlight

Highlight is not always fixed or solid. attention, emphasis, and importance can exist as a gradual shift rather than a clear boundary.

In this version, the highlight fades into the surrounding text. Instead of clearly separating important words, it creates a soft transition. The hierarchy becomes less defined, and reading feels more ambiguous.

This suggests that meaning is not always sharply defined, but can exist in between states of visibility.

Corrupted Reading

We often assume that highlighted text helps us read more clearly, but emphasis can also interrupt, distort, and misdirect attention. A marked phrase does not simply stand out. It changes the rhythm of reading and creates a hierarchy that may or may not reflect meaning.

In this file, the document no longer feels stable. Certain parts remain readable, while others seem extracted, repeated, or displaced. Highlight becomes less like a tool and more like a force acting on the page.

What remains is not a complete text, but a trace of how reading has been altered.

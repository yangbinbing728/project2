Wrong Highlight

We often believe that we read text objectively, but our attention is constantly guided by visual emphasis. Certain words become more important simply because they are highlighted.

In this version, highlight becomes misleading. The system emphasizes the wrong words, so the reading hierarchy feels unstable and confused.

It asks whether highlighting truly helps reading, or whether it can also distort meaning by directing attention in the wrong place.
